Our Services

Rooms


Our rooms are designed with your comfort in mind, offering a relaxing atmosphere, modern amenities, and impeccable cleanliness to ensure a restful stay.

Laundry Service


We offer prompt and professional laundry services for your convenience. Simply place your items in the laundry bag provided and contact the front desk for pickup. Same-day service is available for requests made before 10:00 AM.

Barbing Saloon


Enjoy professional grooming services at our in-house barbing salon. Our skilled barbers offer stylish cuts, trims, and shaves in a clean and comfortable setting. Walk-ins are welcome.

Bar/Restaurant


Enjoy a delicious selection of local and international cuisine at our on-site restaurant, complemented by a wide range of beverages at the bar. Open daily for breakfast, lunch, and dinner in a relaxed and inviting atmosphere.

Cofee/Arabia Tea


Enjoy a freshly brewed cup of coffee or traditional Arabian tea, available daily. A perfect way to start your day or unwind at any time during your stay.

Supermarket


Conveniently located within the hotel premises, our supermarket offers a wide selection of everyday essentials, snacks, beverages, and personal care items to meet your needs during your stay.

Barbeue


Enjoy our delicious barbecue, featuring expertly grilled meats and flavorful sides; perfect for a casual and satisfying meal.

Event Centre


Our Event Centre offers a versatile space perfect for conferences, meetings, weddings, and special occasions, equipped with modern amenities to ensure a successful and memorable event.

Swimming Pool


Enjoy a refreshing swim in our clean, well-maintained swimming pool, perfect for relaxation and recreation during your stay.
